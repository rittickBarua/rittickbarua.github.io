# Rittick Barua, PhD

I'm a product owner and applied data scientist with 7+ years delivering enterprise ML and GenAI systems — taking ideas from discovery through to scaled adoption. I currently work on language-model platforms for the enterprise and on computer-vision pipelines for clinical microscopy. Previously I founded a research-strategy consultancy and led an applied-science team in management consulting. PhD in Engineering, University of Cambridge.

## Selected work

- [**Chat:R**](/work/chat-r/) — an extended LibreChat platform serving ~1,000 staff at a public-affairs consultancy across three Azure environments.
- [**Cell-spheroid segmentation**](/work/cell-spheroid-segmentation/) — a U-Net microscopy pipeline for a surgical-biotechnology research group; validation IoU 0.968 / Dice 0.983.
- [**London Property Finder**](/work/london-property/) — a stacked XGBoost + quantile-regression ensemble on 2.18M Land Registry transactions, scoring live listings.
