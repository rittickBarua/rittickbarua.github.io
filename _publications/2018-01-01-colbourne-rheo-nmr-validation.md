Introduces and validates a portable low-field Rheo-NMR instrument (1H resonance frequency 10.7 MHz) suitable for process monitoring, benchmarked against high-field Rheo-NMR on matched samples across matched shear rates. Applied to measure shear-induced migration of suspended non-colloidal particles in Couette flow.
